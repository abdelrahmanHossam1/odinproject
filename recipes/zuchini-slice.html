zuchini slice recipe

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • onions
  • bacon slices
  • garlic
  • vegetables
  • eggs
  • self-raising flour
  • milk
  • cheese
  • stack powder
  • tomatoes

How to make it

  1. The onions are added to the olive oil first and softened slightly before adding the carrots and garlic.
  2. Once the onions and carrots are soft and golden (a little colour at this point = flavour!), the bacon is added, followed by the zucchini.
  3. Once the vegetable and bacon mix is cooked and liquid has mostly evaporated, it is ready and set aside in the pan to cool slightly.
  4. The flour is added to the slightly cooled vegetables and stirred through.
  5. Then half the egg mixture is added and stirred, then add the remaining eggs and cheese and carefully combine.
  6. The eggs are added in 2 parts as it stops the mixture from forming clumps. Once the mixture is smooth, it is carefully poured into the lined pan.
  7. The slice is then sprinkled with parmesan cheese and topped with thinly sliced tomatoes before being placed in a preheated oven to cook for 25-30 minutes or until firm to the touch.
